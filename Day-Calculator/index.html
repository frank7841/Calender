Exact day of the week Calculator

Do you Know the exact day of the week that you were born?

Most of us know of the date we were born. However, unless our parents told us of the exact day we were born, this information has eluded us. The website takes in your gender, date of birth, month, and year and outputs your Akan name based on the day of the week that you were born. The Akan naming system is a ghanian culture where by children are given their first name depending on their day of birth and gender.
